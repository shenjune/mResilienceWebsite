Mobile App for First Responders

A customizable resilience-building app available 24/7 that provides direct access to peer support, department supported resources, and stress management tools.

First Responder Challenges

First responders put resilience to the test every day. The ability to “bounce back” is critical to do the work of protecting and saving lives. But the challenges of first responder work hit at the core. Strengthening and maintaining resilience are the cornerstone of any prevention program.

Effects of Stress & Trauma

  • Burnout
  • Obesity
  • Substance Abuse
  • PTSD
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Domestic Abuse
  • Suicide
  • Poor cardiac health
  • Agitation/Anger

Department Challenges

  • Impact of stress on productivity, performance and retention
  • Cost-effective ways to address the mental challenges of the job
  • The stigma against seeking psychological help

The Solution

mResilience is a customized program, licensed to first responder agencies to develop, maintain, and rebuild psychological resilience. It’s a combination of in-person training and a companion mobile application.

Why mResilience

  • Use the app is 100% confidential
  • Capitalize on the values of first responders
  • Provides access to department, local and national resources
  • Discreet and personal tools to build and support resiliency
  • Available to spouses, partners and children at no additional cost

Program Offers

  • Customizable, resilience-building mobile app
  • In-person resilience training tailored to department’s needs
  • Administrative web portal for direct, easy updates to key app features
  • Stress management and performance-enhancing tools
  • Ways to connect to Peer Support team members within the department

Vision

  • First Responders can navigate difficult situations and stay resilient
  • Effective stress management and performance-enhancing tools on-the-go
  • Department programs are reinforced through improved access
  • The program helps reverse trends of stress-related challenges for first responders

Update App Content in Minutes!

The mRes Portal is a web application that syncs with the mRes mobile app. Departments can take control by customizing app content anytime. Content changes in the Portal will immediately update to the app at the click of a button.

  • Update department Peer Support roster including adding/deleting team members, modifying bios, and changing contact information
  • Configure department, local and national resources to fit employee needs
  • Update wellness tips to share with employees (coming soon!)
